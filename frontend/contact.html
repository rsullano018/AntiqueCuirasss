Frequently Asked Questions

How do you authenticate your artifacts?

All our artifacts undergo rigorous authentication by our team of expert historians and military specialists. We provide detailed certificates of authenticity and provenance documentation for every piece.

Do you offer international shipping?

Yes, we ship worldwide with full insurance and tracking. International shipping costs and delivery times vary by location. Contact us for specific shipping quotes.

What is your return policy?

We offer a 30-day return policy for all purchases. Items must be returned in their original condition with all documentation. Custom authentication services are non-refundable.

Do you buy artifacts from collectors?

Yes, we actively purchase authentic military antiques from private collectors. Contact our authentication team to discuss your items and receive a professional appraisal.

How long does authentication take?

Standard authentication typically takes 5-7 business days. Complex items requiring additional research may take up to 2 weeks. Rush services are available for an additional fee.

Do you offer payment plans?

Yes, we offer flexible payment plans for purchases over $1,000. Contact us to discuss available options and terms that work for your budget.
